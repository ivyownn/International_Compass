About International Compass

International Compass is an outreach ministry of Revival International, created to support international students as they transition to life in the United States. Our mission is to connect students with essential resources, mentorship, and a caring church family – helping them settle in smoothly and thrive in a new environment.

Revival International is one of the denominations under the United Denominations – Originating from the Lighthouse Group of Churches (UD-OLGC), founded by Bishop Dag Heward-Mills. Based in the U.S. with active branches along the East Coast, we are a vibrant, youth-driven community committed to sharing the love of Christ through service, connection, and compassion.

Through International Compass, we extend that mission by offering both practical and spiritual support to international students. Whether it is airport pickups, cultural guidance, or one-on-one mentoring, our goal is to walk alongside students with faith and friendship. We share this vision with our sister churches — First Love, Laikos International Church, and the American Missionary Church (AMC) — which serve communities across the U.S. and Canada, united in our commitment to love, purpose, and excellence.

We focus on spiritual, academic, and cultural needs, helping you find a true home away from home.
