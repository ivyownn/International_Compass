How We Serve

At International Compass, we’re here to help international students feel at home, grow in faith, and build strong support systems during their time in the U.S. Below are some of the services we offer:

✈️ Airport Pickups

Arriving in a new country can be overwhelming. Let us know your flight details and we’ll help make your arrival smooth and welcoming.

🏠 Church Family Connections

We help students find a local Revival International or sister church near their campus to stay spiritually grounded and make new friends.

🤝 Mentorship

Get connected with someone who’s been in your shoes! Our mentors offer support with academics, spiritual growth, and adjusting to U.S. culture.

🌎 Cultural Orientation

Need help understanding how things work here—from opening a bank account to understanding public transport? We’re here for that too.

🚗 Request a Ride to Church

Need a ride to church on Sunday or a midweek service? We’ve got you covered! Just let us know in advance, and someone from our team will arrange transportation for you.
Request a ride here.

🧡 Community & Events

Join us for hangouts, game nights, worship services, and other events that help students connect and build lasting friendships.

Want to sign up for one of our services or learn more? Reach out here or stop by one of our events!
