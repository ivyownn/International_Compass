Welcome to International Compass

Your journey starts here!

Moving to a new country is exciting, but we know it can bring challenges, too. That’s why International Compass is more than just a resource—it’s a supportive community ready to help you every step of the way. Do you need guidance getting from the airport? Are you looking for friends who feel like family? Or if you’re just curious about navigating campus life, you’re in the right place!

Dive in and discover a world of resources, connections, and insider tips designed to make your transition smoother and more memorable. Together, let’s make your college experience in the U.S. unforgettable!

Learn more about how International Compass is here to guide and support you as you embark on this exciting journey.
